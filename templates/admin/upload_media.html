1. Open any YouTube video
2. Click Share button below the video
3. Click Copy link — looks like: https://youtu.be/VIDEOID
4. OR copy from address bar: https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=VIDEOID
Paste the full YouTube URL or just the Video ID
